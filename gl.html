Hazard identification in a virtual worksite



An experience about the navigation techniques in a virtual world

Purpose of the study

This experiment aims to evaluate some navigation techniques for the specific task of hazard identification.
You will have to use 2 different techniques, ONLY 1 time for each technique. Repeat the experiment ONLY in case of a severe bug, such as an infinite move of the player.

Preliminary steps

  1. Download and read this informative PDF about fall and struck-by hazards, available here.
  2. Read the tutorial about the application and the commands.
  3. Fill and submit the questionnaire.

The experience in the virtual worksite

What I have to do ?

  • First you will be able to practise in the application, by moving around the building and identifying a "fake hazard":
    • First, using a "direct" navigation technique -> I click on the ground and then I move there.
    • Secondly, using a "indirect" navigation technique -> I click on the map and then I move.

  • Then, you will experiment these two techniques in two real scenarios
    • You must navigate around the building: check the 4 facades, and come back to the starting point.
    • During this inspection, you must identify the workers that are in an hazardous situation.
  • The central building is the one which is currently in construction during the worksite. The buildings around it are already finished.
  • The experiment starts in a corner of the central building. Move around the central building, along its 4 facades.
  • You must check the 4 facades, and come back to the starting point.
  • When you have came back, click on the red parallelepiped to end the game. Please, don't forget it otherwise your completion task time will be false.
